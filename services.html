Our NGINX Services at Ondia Academy

At Ondia Academy, we specialize in providing comprehensive NGINX solutions. Whether you're looking to optimize your current setup, implement new features, or require expert consultation, our team is here to help you leverage the full power of NGINX.

NGINX Configuration & Setup

We offer professional NGINX installation and configuration services tailored to your specific needs. This includes setting up server blocks (virtual hosts), SSL/TLS termination, and basic optimizations for new or existing web applications.

Performance Optimization

Maximize your website's speed and efficiency with our NGINX performance tuning services. We analyze your setup and implement best practices for caching, compression, connection handling, and content delivery to ensure optimal performance.

Security Hardening

Protect your web infrastructure with NGINX security hardening. Our services include implementing security headers, rate limiting, access controls, Web Application Firewall (WAF) integration (e.g., ModSecurity), and mitigation against common web vulnerabilities.

Load Balancing & High Availability

Ensure your applications are resilient and can handle high traffic loads. We design and implement robust NGINX load balancing solutions (HTTP, TCP/UDP) and high availability setups to distribute traffic efficiently and prevent single points of failure.

NGINX Training & Workshops

Empower your team with the knowledge to manage and optimize NGINX effectively. We provide customized training sessions and workshops covering everything from NGINX fundamentals to advanced topics and best practices.

Reverse Proxy & API Gateway Solutions

Utilize NGINX as a powerful reverse proxy for your microservices, legacy applications, or as an API gateway. We can help you configure routing, request/response manipulation, authentication, and more.
